    Stack Blocks Three


    Description: There are three blocks on the table, the color of the blocks is red, green and blue. Move the blocks to the center of the table, and stack the blue block on the green block, and the green block on the red block.
    Average Steps: 481 (Aloha-AgileX, save_freq=15)
    Objects: block
    Embodiments Aloha-AgileX ARX-X5 Franka-Panda Piper UR5-Wsg
    Success Rate 94% 96% 80% 0% 51%
